How Our Donation System Works

Every round, our community votes to determine which charity receives the accumulated funds from our PumpFun trading fees. The process is completely transparent and automated, ensuring that 100% of designated funds reach the chosen charities.

Rounds run 24/7 with increasing durations: starting at 5 minutes for Round 1, then 10, 20, 30 minutes, and capping at 1 hour. This allows for quick initial engagement while building up to larger donation amounts in longer rounds.
