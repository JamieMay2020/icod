Frequently Asked Questions

How does the voting system work? +
Each round, you can vote for one charity from the five presented options. Voting is completely anonymous - no sign-up or wallet connection required. When the timer reaches zero, the charity with the most votes receives the donation.
How are charities selected for each round? +
We maintain a pool of 10 verified charities. Each round displays 5 charities. The winner automatically stays in the next round, while the 3 charities with the least votes are rotated out and replaced with others from the pool.
How long does each round last? +
Round durations increase progressively: 5 minutes, 10 minutes, 20 minutes, 30 minutes, then cap at 1 hour. This allows for quick initial rounds while giving more time for larger donations in later rounds.
Where do the funds come from? +
All donations come from the fees generated by trading our token on PumpFun. 100% of these fees go directly to the winning charities - we don't take any cut.
How can I verify the donations? +
Every donation is documented on our Past Donations page with links to transaction proofs and public announcements. We maintain complete transparency about where every dollar goes.
Can I vote multiple times? +
You can vote once per round. Our system tracks anonymous voting to ensure fairness while protecting your privacy. Once a new round starts, you can vote again.
